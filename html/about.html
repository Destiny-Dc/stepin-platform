About KTU

Koforidua Technical University (KTU) is a public tertiary institution located in the Eastern Region of Ghana. It is committed to training skilled professionals in engineering, applied sciences, and business disciplines. KTU offers hands-on, industry-oriented education to prepare students for real-world challenges and innovations.

About StepIn

StepIn is a digital orientation platform created to help new students navigate life at KTU with ease. It provides announcements, video resources, interactive FAQs, and live sessions to guide you through your academic and social journey at the university. StepIn is your first step to feeling at home at KTU.
